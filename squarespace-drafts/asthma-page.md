## Iframe Embed Code

Paste this into a **Code Block** in Squarespace:

```html
<iframe
  src="https://rikhardfi.github.io/clinical-wiki/?embed=true&page=asthma"
  style="width:100%;min-height:1800px;border:none;"
```

## Notes
- Adjust `min-height` as needed (1800px should cover all sections)
- The `?embed=true` parameter hides the navigation bar
- The iframe inherits the page's scroll — no double scrollbars
- Works within Member Areas (auth handled by Squarespace, iframe content is public)
